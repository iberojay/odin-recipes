Rum and Coke

\

Description

Refreshing rum and coke that will get you waved.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces light or dark rum
  • 4 to 6 ounces of Coca-Cola, to taste
  • Lime wedge, for garnish

Directions

  1. Pour 2 ounces of the rum into a glass filled with ice.
  2. Top with cola, to taste.
  3. Garnish with lime wedge. Slam it and enjoy.
