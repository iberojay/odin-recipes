Smash Burger

Description

This smash burger recipe makes juicy burgers with crispy edges.

Ingredients

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 2 tablespoons of butter (softened)
  • 1 pound of ground beef (80% lean)
  • 4 (6-inch) squares of parchment paper
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 slices of American cheese
  • Burger toppings of choice

Directions

  1. Preheat stove top for high heat and set cast iron flat-top griddle or large large cast iron skillet onto stove and preheat until smoking.
  2. Spread butter on the insides of the buns and toast until lightly browned
  3. Form meat into 8 loosely-packed balls, 2 ounces each. Don't pack the meat tightly, as this will prevent it from smashing properly. Place each ball on the the pan and cover meat with a piece of parchemnt paper (prevents sticking of spatuala). Immediately smash down to a 1/4 inch thickness using 2 stiff, sturdy spatulas that are criss-crossed to get proper leverage as you press down.Afterwards sprinle meat with salt.
  4. Let the meat cook for about 45 seconds, until the edges are dark brownedand the centers are a light pink colour. Using a spatuala, gently scrape up the patties, flip opver and immediatley cover 4 of them with cheese. Cook for an additional 15 to 20 seconds; stack the plain patties over the cheese-covered patties so you have 4 stacks. Move each stack to a bun and serve with your favourite toppings.
